Meet the Illustrator!

Hi there!
I'm Maximiliano, a comic's illustrator and digital artist. Currently, I'm based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I've been working as an artist for the past 4 years, from characters design and portraits to personalized commissions. Currently, I'm working on my own comic book project named 'Wolf & Sord'.

I can do concept art, visual development and final illustration in a wide range of styles.
If you are looking for a dedicated professional with the commitment to troubleshooting and willing to push the boundaries of imagination, stop now and here! I'll be glad to help you!
